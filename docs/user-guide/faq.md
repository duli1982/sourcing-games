# FAQ & Troubleshooting

Frequently asked questions and solutions to common issues.

## Account & Login

**Q: I forgot my PIN. What do I do?**
A: Contact platform support to reset your PIN. For security, PINs cannot be recovered, only reset.

**Q: Can I change my username?**
A: Not currently. Choose your name carefully during registration.

**Q: How do I delete my account?**
A: Contact support for account deletion requests (GDPR compliant).

## Games & Submissions

**Q: Why is my submission taking a long time to score?**
A: AI scoring takes 5-10 seconds. If longer, check your connection and refresh.

**Q: I disagree with my score. Can I appeal?**
A: Scores use consistent rubrics. Review feedback and retry to improve.

**Q: Can I edit a submission after submitting?**
A: No, but you can submit again. Your best score is recorded.

**Q: How many games are there?**
A: Multiple individual and team games in rotating collections, all remain available.

## Teams

**Q: How do I find a team to join?**
A: Ask other players for their team join code, or create your own team.

**Q: Can I be kicked out of a team?**
A: Team admins have member management capabilities.

**Q: What is the difference between Teams Leaderboard and Team Games Leaderboard?**
A: Teams Leaderboard uses individual game scores. Team Games Leaderboard uses collaborative team submissions.

## Leaderboards

**Q: How often do leaderboards update?**
A: Real-time after every submission.

**Q: Why did my rank drop even though I improved?**
A: Leaderboards are relative. Other players improving affects your rank.

## Technical Issues

**Q: The page is not loading. What should I do?**
Try: Refresh, clear cache, try different browser, check connection.

**Q: I see an error when submitting.**
Common errors: Submission too short, network error, server error, invalid format.

## Scoring & Feedback

**Q: What is a good score?**
- 90-100: Excellent
- 80-89: Very Good
- 70-79: Good
- 60-69: Fair
- Below 60: Needs Improvement

**Q: How is the rubric applied?**
Each game has specific criteria weighted by points. AI evaluates against each criterion.

## Team Games

**Q: When do new team games appear?**
A: Bi-weekly on the 15th and end of each month.

**Q: Can I submit a team game for multiple teams?**
A: No, submissions are for your active team only.

## Best Practices

**Q: What is the best way to improve my scores?**
1. Read AI feedback carefully
2. Practice regularly
3. Learn from mistakes
4. Focus on weak areas
5. Use Learning Hub resources

**Q: Should I focus on easy or hard games?**
A: Balanced approach. All difficulties contribute equally to total score.

## Data & Privacy

**Q: What data does the platform collect?**
A: Player names, PINs (hashed), submissions, scores, session data. See privacy policy.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes. Session tokens in HttpOnly cookies, PINs hashed, secure Supabase storage.

**Q: Can other players see my submissions?**
A: No. Submissions are private. Only scores and names are public.

## Getting Help

**Q: I have a question not answered here.**
A: Contact platform support through the in-platform help feature.

**Q: How do I report a bug?**
A: Contact support with description, steps to reproduce, and screenshots.

**Q: Can I suggest new features?**
A: Yes! Contact support with your ideas.
