# Leaderboards Guide

Leaderboards showcase top performers across individual games, team competitions, and team game challenges.

## Understanding the Leaderboards

### Three Leaderboard Types

1. **Individual Leaderboard**: Personal performance in individual games
2. **Teams Leaderboard**: Team performance based on member contributions
3. **Team Games Leaderboard**: Team performance in collaborative challenges

## Individual Leaderboard

### How It Works

Ranks all players based on their individual game performance.

**Score Calculation:**
- Sum of your best scores across all individual games
- Only your highest score per game counts
- Updates in real-time after each submission

**Displayed Information:**
- Rank, player name, total score, games played

### Improving Your Rank

- Play more games
- Improve existing scores
- Apply AI feedback
- Aim for 90+ scores consistently

## Teams Leaderboard

### How It Works

Ranks teams based on aggregate member performance in individual games.

**Score Calculation:**
- Sum of all team members best individual game scores
- Each member contributes their personal bests
- More active members = higher total score

## Team Games Leaderboard

### How It Works

Ranks teams based on collaborative team game performance.

**Score Calculation:**
- Sum of team best scores across all team games
- Only team game submissions count
- Individual games do NOT affect this leaderboard

## Viewing Leaderboards

Access via Leaderboard in main navigation. Features include:
- Search and filter capabilities
- Your position highlighted
- Real-time updates
- Top 50 displayed by default

## Ranking Mechanics

### Tie Breaking

When scores are equal, ordered by:
- Number of games played
- Team member count (for teams)
- Recent activity

### Updates

All leaderboards update in real-time after submissions.

## Competitive Strategies

### Individual Competition

- Play all available games
- Replay low-scoring games
- Aim for 90+ on every game
- Focus on weak skill areas
- Play regularly for consistency

### Team Competition

For Teams Leaderboard:
- Recruit active, skilled members
- Encourage daily participation
- Share strategies within team

For Team Games Leaderboard:
- Coordinate bi-weekly challenges
- Submit multiple refined attempts
- Demonstrate strong collaboration

## Understanding Your Performance

Check your profile for:
- Total score and rank
- Games played count
- Average score per game
- Skill distribution
- Recent activity trends

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How often are leaderboards updated?**
A: In real-time after every submission.

**Q: Can I see historical leaderboard data?**
A: Currently only current rankings are shown.

**Q: What happens if I am on multiple teams?**
A: Your individual scores contribute to all teams.

**Q: Can teams drop in ranking?**
A: Yes, as other teams improve, relative rankings change.

**Q: Do team game scores affect the Teams Leaderboard?**
A: No, team games have their own separate leaderboard.
