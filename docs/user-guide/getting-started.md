# Getting Started with Sourcing Games

Welcome to Sourcing Games! This guide will help you get started and make the most of the platform.

## Creating Your Account

### First-Time Registration

1. **Visit the Platform**: Navigate to the Sourcing Games website
2. **Enter Your Name**: Provide your full name or preferred display name
3. **Set Your PIN**: Create a 4-digit security PIN for account access
   - This PIN protects your account and progress
   - Keep it secure - you'll need it every time you log in
   - If you forget your PIN, you'll need to contact support

4. **Complete Registration**: Click "Sign Up" to create your account

### Logging In

For returning users:

1. **Enter Your Name**: Type the exact name you registered with
2. **Enter Your PIN**: Provide your 4-digit security PIN
3. **Click "Login"**: Access your account and continue your progress

> **Tip**: Your name and PIN combination is unique. Make sure to remember both!

## Understanding the Dashboard

After logging in, you'll see the main dashboard with several key sections:

### Navigation Menu

- **Home**: Overview and quick access to game tracks
- **Individual Games**: Daily solo challenges
- **Team Games**: Bi-weekly team challenges
- **Teams**: Create, join, and manage teams
- **Leaderboard**: View global rankings (Individual, Teams, Team Games)
- **Profile**: Your stats, recent attempts, and progress
- **Learning Hub**: Educational resources and guides
- **Coach**: AI-powered feedback system

### Home Page Overview

The home page presents two main tracks:

#### Individual Games Track
- Daily rotation of sourcing challenges
- Solo submissions with personal scoring
- AI feedback on every attempt
- Personal leaderboard ranking

#### Team Games Track
- Bi-weekly challenges (15th and end of month)
- Collaborative team submissions
- Multi-platform sourcing focus
- Team leaderboard competition

## Your First Game

### Playing an Individual Game

1. **Navigate to Games**: Click "Individual Games" from the menu
2. **Read the Challenge**: Each game presents:
   - A title and description
   - Specific task requirements
   - Difficulty level (Easy, Medium, Hard)
   - Skill category (Boolean, Outreach, Talent Intelligence, etc.)

3. **Craft Your Solution**: 
   - Type your answer in the submission box
   - Follow the task instructions carefully
   - Use the placeholder text as a guide

4. **Submit for Scoring**:
   - Click "Submit" to send your answer
   - Wait for AI Coach to analyze your submission
   - Receive instant feedback and score (0-100 points)

5. **Review Feedback**:
   - Read the detailed analysis
   - Understand your score breakdown
   - Learn from suggestions for improvement
   - Try again to beat your score!

### Understanding Scoring

Every submission is scored on a 0-100 point scale based on:

- **Criteria-Based Rubric**: Each game has specific evaluation criteria
  - Easy games: 4 criteria (20-30 points each)
  - Medium games: 4 criteria (20-30 points each)
  - Hard games: 4 criteria (20-30 points each)

- **AI Analysis**: The AI Coach evaluates your submission against:
  - Keyword relevance
  - Boolean logic (if applicable)
  - Platform-specific syntax
  - Completeness and optimization
  - Strategic thinking

- **Best Score Counts**: Your highest score for each game is saved
- **Total Score**: Sum of your best scores across all games

## Joining a Team

Teams unlock collaborative challenges and team-based competition:

1. **Navigate to Teams**: Click "Teams" from the menu
2. **Create or Join**:
   - **Create Team**: Start your own team with a unique name
   - **Join Team**: Use a team's join code to become a member

3. **Team Membership**:
   - You can be a member of multiple teams
   - Set one team as your "active team" for submissions
   - Team admins can manage members

## Common First-Time Questions

### How often do games change?
- **Individual Games**: Daily rotation with new challenges
- **Team Games**: Bi-weekly rotation (15th and end of each month)

### Can I retry a game?
Yes! You can attempt each game as many times as you want. Your best score is recorded.

### How is my ranking calculated?
Your rank is based on:
- **Individual Leaderboard**: Total score (sum of best scores across all individual games)
- **Team Leaderboard**: Team's total score (sum of all members' individual game scores)
- **Team Games Leaderboard**: Team's best scores in team game challenges

### What if I make a mistake during registration?
Contact support to update your account information or reset your PIN.

### Do I need to be on a team to play?
No! Teams are optional. You can enjoy individual games without joining a team. However, teams unlock additional challenges and collaborative learning.
