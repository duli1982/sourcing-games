# Teams Management Guide

Teams enable collaborative learning and competition through team games and shared progress tracking.

## Creating a Team

### How to Create a Team

1. Navigate to the **Teams** page from the main menu
2. Click **Create Team** button
3. Enter a unique team name
4. Click **Create** to finalize

### Team Name Guidelines

- Must be unique across the platform
- Choose something memorable and professional
- Cannot be changed after creation
- Represents your team on leaderboards

### Team Creator Role

As the creator, you become the team admin with special privileges:
- Manage team members
- Accept/remove members (coming soon)
- Represent the team in team games

## Joining a Team

### How to Join

1. Get a team join code from the team admin or existing member
2. Navigate to **Teams** page
3. Click **Join Team** button
4. Enter the join code
5. Click **Join** to become a member

### Join Codes

- Each team has a unique join code
- Share it securely with intended members only
- Anyone with the code can join the team
- Contact team admin if you need the code

### Membership Benefits

Team members can:
- Participate in team games
- Submit on behalf of the team
- View team progress and stats
- Contribute to team leaderboard score
- Collaborate with teammates

## Managing Multiple Teams

### Being on Multiple Teams

You can join multiple teams simultaneously. This allows you to:
- Participate in different team games
- Collaborate with different groups
- Compare team strategies
- Learn from various approaches

### Active Team

- Only one team can be active at a time
- Team game submissions count for your active team
- Individual games always count for you personally

### Switching Active Team

1. Go to **Teams** page
2. Find the team you want to activate
3. Click **Set as Active Team**
4. Confirm the team is now highlighted as active

## Team Page Features

### My Teams Tab

View all teams you are a member of:
- Team name and member count
- Your role (Admin or Member)
- Team total score
- Team games played
- Quick access to team details

### Team Detail Page

Click on any team to see:
- **Team Stats**: Total score, games played, team ranking
- **Team Members**: List of all members with roles
- **Recent Attempts**: Latest team game submissions
- **Best Scores**: Highest scores per team game
- **Team Progress**: Performance over time

### Leaderboard Tab

View global team rankings:
- Top teams by total score
- Team scores and games played
- Your team ranking highlighted
- Real-time updates

## Team Roles

### Admin (Team Creator)

Responsibilities:

- Represent team in competitions

Privileges:
- Full team access
- Future: Member management capabilities

### Member

Responsibilities:
- Participate in team games
- Coordinate with team on submissions
- Contribute to team success
- Follow team guidelines

Privileges:
- Submit team games
- View team progress
- Access team stats

## Team Scoring

### Individual Games Team Score

Your team total score from individual games is:
- Sum of all team members best scores across individual games
- Each member contributes their personal best scores
- More active members = higher team score
- Updates automatically as members play

### Team Games Score

Separate scoring for collaborative team games:
- Sum of team best scores across team game challenges
- Only counts team game submissions
- One submission per team per game
- Best attempt is recorded

### Leaderboards

**Teams Leaderboard:**
- Ranks teams by total individual game scores
- Sum of all members personal bests

**Team Games Leaderboard:**
- Ranks teams by total team game scores
- Sum of best team game attempts

## Best Practices

### Team Coordination

- **Communication**: Set up a communication channel (email, Slack, Discord)
- **Scheduling**: Coordinate team game submissions
- **Roles**: Assign responsibilities based on strengths
- **Review**: Establish peer review process
- **Learning**: Share feedback and insights

### Effective Teamwork

- **Regular Check-ins**: Stay connected with team members
- **Clear Responsibilities**: Know who does what
- **Respect Deadlines**: Be reliable for team submissions
- **Collaborative Review**: Review together before submitting
- **Celebrate Wins**: Acknowledge team successes

### Team Growth

- **Recruit Actively**: Invite skilled sourcers to join
- **Diversity**: Include members with different strengths
- **Engagement**: Keep team motivated and active
- **Improvement**: Learn from feedback together
- **Consistency**: Maintain regular participation

## Team Strategy Tips

### For Team Games

- **Divide & Conquer**: Split complex tasks among members
- **Leverage Strengths**: Assign based on expertise
- **Quality Control**: Peer review before submission
- **Iterate**: Submit multiple times to improve
- **Document**: Keep track of what works

### For Individual Contribution

- **Stay Active**: Play individual games regularly
- **Aim High**: Higher individual scores boost team total
- **Share Strategies**: Help teammates improve
- **Consistency**: Regular play compounds team benefits

## Leaving a Team

If you need to leave a team:
1. Contact the team admin
2. Admin can remove you from the team (coming soon)
3. For now, you can simply set a different team as active

Note: Your contributed scores remain with the team even after leaving.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How many teams can I join?**
A: Unlimited! You can be a member of as many teams as you want.

**Q: Can I play team games for multiple teams?**
A: Only for your active team. Switch active teams to play for different teams.

**Q: Do my individual game scores count for all my teams?**
A: Yes! Your individual scores contribute to all teams you are a member of.

**Q: What happens if I leave a team?**
A: Your past contributions remain, but future individual scores will not count for that team.

**Q: Can teams have the same name?**
A: No, team names must be unique across the platform.

**Q: How do I find my team join code?**
A: Ask your team admin or an existing member who has access.

**Q: Can I rename my team?**
A: Not currently. Choose your team name carefully when creating.

**Q: What is the maximum team size?**
A: No limit! Teams can have as many members as desired.
