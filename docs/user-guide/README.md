## What is Sourcing Games?

Sourcing Games is a gamified learning platform designed to help recruiters and talent sourcers master essential skills through hands-on practice. The platform offers:

- **Daily Individual Challenges**: Practice Boolean search, outreach strategies, and talent intelligence
- **Team Competitions**: Collaborate on advanced multi-platform sourcing challenges
- **AI-Powered Feedback**: Get instant, personalized feedback on every submission
- **Global Leaderboards**: Compete with peers worldwide and track your progress
- **Skill Development**: Build expertise across multiple sourcing platforms (LinkedIn, GitHub, Stack Overflow, Reddit)

## Key Features

### Individual Games
Test your skills with daily rotating challenges across multiple categories:
- Boolean Search Construction
- Outreach Strategy Development
- Talent Intelligence Analysis
- LinkedIn Sourcing Techniques
- And more...

Each game is scored on a 0-100 point scale with detailed AI feedback to help you improve.

### Team Games
Collaborate with your team on bi-weekly challenges (15th and end of each month):
- Multi-platform sourcing (GitHub, Stack Overflow, Reddit)
- Advanced strategic challenges
- Team-based scoring and leaderboards
- Collaborative learning opportunities

### Learning Hub
Access curated learning resources to enhance your sourcing skills:
- Platform-specific guides
- Best practices and strategies
- Community-contributed content
- Expert tips and techniques

### AI Coach
Get personalized feedback powered by Gemini AI:
- Detailed analysis of your submissions
- Actionable improvement suggestions
- Scoring based on professional rubrics
- Real-time feedback on every attempt
